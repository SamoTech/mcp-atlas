# Governance: Evidence Policy

## Scoring rules
- Anonymous case studies never score above 2/5.
- Press-release-only claims never score above 4/5 without workflow detail.
- Missing governance detail lowers the score.
- Marketing language is translated into architecture language before publication.

## Inference rules
- Directly stated facts are written as facts.
- Plausible architecture interpretations are labeled as inference.
- Unknown details are kept visible instead of filled with optimistic assumptions.

## Correction policy
Any contributor can submit a correction, but a score change requires public evidence.
